Move In/Out Cleaning Service

Whether you’re moving into a new home or moving out of an old one, our move in/out cleaning service ensures that the space is left spotless.

  • Basic Home Cleaning
  • Move In/Out Cleaning

What’s Included:

  • Deep cleaning of all surfaces, floors, and carpets
  • Detailed cleaning of kitchens and bathrooms
  • Cleaning inside cabinets, drawers, and closets
  • Cleaning windows, baseboards, and light fixtures
  • Removal of all dust and debris

Living Room:

  • Dust and clean ceiling fans and external lamps.
  • Clean all dust.
  • Wash all windows.
  • Vacuum and mop floors.

Bedrooms:

  • Dust and clean all external lamps and fans.
  • Dust and wipe clean all surfaces – cupboards and drawers.
  • Vacuum and mop floors. Throw away rubbish.

Bathrooms:

  • Clean all dust.
  • Wash windows.
  • Wipe mirror and door. Scrub tile.
  • Clean faucets, sinks, bathtub, and shower.
  • Clean all cabinets from inside and outside.
  • Vacuum and mop floors.

Kitchen:

  • Dust and clean all external lamps.
  • Clean inside and outside of cabinets, sinks.
  • Wipe all surfaces – countertops and tables.
  • Vacuum and mop floors.
