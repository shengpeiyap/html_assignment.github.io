Basic Home Cleaning Service

Our basic home cleaning service is perfect for homeowners who want to keep their homes fresh and tidy on a regular basis.

  • Basic Home Cleaning
  • Move In/Out Cleaning

What’s Included:

  • General cleaning of all rooms
  • Dusting of surfaces, shelves, and furniture
  • Vacuuming and mopping floors
  • Kitchen and bathroom sanitation
  • Trash removal and recycling

Living Room:

  • Dust and wipe clean all surfaces, including coffee tables and cabinets, leaving them spotless and gleaming.
  • Organise furniture and decoration items.
  • Vacuum and mop floors.
  • Clear all your rubbish bins.

Bedrooms:

  • Change your bedsheet and make up your bed.
  • Organise furniture, items and clothes.
  • Dust and wipe clean all surfaces – cupboards and drawers.
  • Vacuum and mop floors.
  • Throw away rubbish.

Bathrooms:

  • Wipe clean all surfaces – counters and cabinets.
  • Clean mirrors, bath, showers and shower doors.
  • Scrub bathroom sinks and toilet bowl.
  • Mop floors and throw away rubbish.

Kitchen:

  • Wash your dirty plates and kitchen utensils.
  • Wipe clean all surfaces – kitchen cupboards, sinks, stove and countertops.
  • Clean the outside of oven, microwave, and fridge.
  • Vacuum and mop floors.
  • Clear all your rubbish bins.
